Chapter 19 Glossary of terms

Term Description
ALK Age length key
OM Operating model
EM Estimation model
AF Age frequency, numbers or proportions in each age cohort
LF Length frequency, numbers or proportions in each model length bin
ABC Acceptable Biological Catch
IFQ Individual Fishing Quotas
NPFMC North Pacific Fishery Management Council
metapopulation A metapopulation as a system of discrete local populations, each of which determines its own internal dynamics to a large extent, but with a degree of identifiable and nontrivial demographic influence from other local populations through dispersal of individuals (Kritzer and Sale 2004)
Connectivity Movement of individuals among geographic areas at any life stage (e.g., larva or adult).
Panmictic A single, unit population with no spatial heterogeneity

References

Kritzer, Jacob P, and Peter F Sale. 2004. “Metapopulation Ecology in the Sea: From Levins’ Model to Marine Ecology and Fisheries Science.” Fish and Fisheries 5 (2): 131–40.
